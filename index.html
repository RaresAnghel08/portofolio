CCIR · Harvard IQSS · MIT

Academic Research · 2025

"Class Bias in LLM-Based Resume Screening: How Socioeconomic Status Signals Shape Scores for Equally Qualified Candidates"

10,000 synthetic resumes generated via Gemini API, scored across Claude Sonnet, Gemini Flash, and local Ollama models (Qwen 2.5 7B, Mistral 7B). OLS/LPM analysis producing 6 publication-quality figures per job type. Key finding: Claude Sonnet shows strong class bias (AMCE ≈ +2.75 pts for employer prestige); Gemini near-zero; Qwen selective.

Supervised by Dr. Danny Ebanks (Harvard IQSS) & Dominic Skinnion (MIT)
